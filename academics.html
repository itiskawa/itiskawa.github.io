Topics I am currently interested in / working on

I am currently working on diffusion-based sampling algorithms. This includes the study of
  • Random Matrix Theory (a pleasant surprise)
  • Spin Glasses & Statistichal Mechanics in general
  • Approximate Message Passing Algorithms
I am quite interested in various fields, all mostly related to emergent structure or 'regularity' in seemingly random structures. That includes Condensation Phenomena, High-Dimensional Statistics, Statistical Physics and more. Information Theory is also a big love of mine, as well as its Quantum counterpart.
